Server Types That Convert

Not all Discord servers are equal. Some are goldmines. Most are wastelands. Here's what separates them:

Dating and Relationship Servers

Members are actively seeking romantic connections. They're emotionally available, primed for attention, and already comfortable with the idea of pursuing someone online. Average conversion rate sits around 15-25% with proper scripts.

Mental Health and Lonely Servers

Communities built around r/lonely, depression support, and social anxiety. These users are desperate for human connection. They form attachments faster, pay for ongoing attention, and rarely question authenticity. Handle accordingly.

Gaming Community Servers

Large servers (100k+ members) have significant populations of men who've never received female attention. Target the "looking for duo" channels and late-night voice chats. Valorant, League, and Minecraft communities work best.

E-girl and Simp Servers

Users here are pre-qualified. They understand the transaction. Competition is higher but so is buyer intent. These aren't people you need to convince—they're people you need to outcompete.

Finding Servers

Public directories are your starting point. Disboard, Discord.me, and Top.gg all have searchable databases. Keywords that work: dating, lonely, egirl, 18+, social, relationship.

Join servers with 1,000+ members and visible activity in the last hour. Dead servers waste time. The Protocol includes a curated, health-rated list updated weekly—saves you the sorting.

Discord-Specific Tactics

  • Never cold DM. Build presence in public channels first. React to messages, participate in conversations, become a recognized name.
  • Use aged accounts. New accounts trigger suspicion. Buy aged accounts or build your own over 2+ weeks before deploying.
  • Avoid mass-DM patterns. Discord's spam detection is sophisticated. Target selectively, space out messages, vary your openers.
  • Persona consistency matters. Your Discord profile, message style, and backstory need to align. Inconsistencies get you caught.
