What is Ewhoring?

Ewhoring is creating fake female online personas to extract money from men on social platforms. It involves social engineering, psychology, and digital marketing. People have been doing this since 2010, and it still works in 2025 because lonely men will always pay for female attention.

Step-by-Step: How to Start Ewhoring

1Set Up Your Anonymous Identity

Never use your real information. You need:

  • VPN - Hide your real IP address (NordVPN, Mullvad)
  • Anonymous email - ProtonMail or Tutanota
  • Burner phone number - TextNow, Google Voice
  • Separate browser - Firefox with privacy extensions

Time: 30 minutes

2Create Your Persona

Build a believable female identity:

  • Name - Common but not generic (Emma, Sophie, Mia)
  • Age - 18-24 works best
  • Location - Match your target timezone
  • Photos - AI-generated or purchased packs (never Google images)
  • Backstory - Keep it simple and consistent

Time: 1-2 hours

3Choose Your Platforms

Where to find targets:

  • Discord - Dating servers, lonely servers, gaming communities
  • Telegram - Premium groups, crypto communities
  • Tinder/Bumble - Direct gift card farming
  • Reddit - Build credibility, funnel to other platforms
  • Snapchat - Premium subscriptions

Time: 1-2 hours to set up profiles

4Start Conversations

Engagement strategy:

  • Build rapport in public channels first (Discord)
  • Never be the first to DM - let them come to you
  • Be slightly unavailable - scarcity creates value
  • Use emotional triggers - loneliness, validation, desire
  • Escalate slowly - don't ask for money immediately

Ongoing - 2-4 hours daily

5Monetize

Convert attention into money:

  • Gift cards - Amazon, Steam, iTunes (safest)
  • Crypto - Bitcoin, USDT (untraceable)
  • CashApp - Quick but can be reversed
  • Premium Snapchat - Monthly subscriptions

Rule: ALWAYS payment before sending anything.

⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes:
  • Using reverse-searchable images
  • Being too eager/desperate
  • Sending content before payment
  • Using the same persona across platforms
  • Not having a consistent backstory
