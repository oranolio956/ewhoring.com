Factors That Affect Earnings

What Increases Earnings:

  • Social skills - Can you hold a conversation?
  • Time investment - More hours = more money
  • Platform selection - Telegram > Discord for ticket size
  • Persona quality - Believable personas convert better
  • Script quality - Tested scripts outperform winging it
  • Targeting - Right audience = easier conversions

What Kills Earnings:

  • Impatience - Asking for money too fast
  • Bad images - Reverse-searchable or low quality
  • Inconsistency - Gaps in persona story
  • Wrong platforms - Dead or over-saturated spaces
  • Laziness - Not following up, not scaling
